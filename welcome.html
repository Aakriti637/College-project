Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my login credentials?

Your login credentials are provided by your institution's administration. Students and parents will receive their username and password via email or during registration. Contact your institution's help desk if you haven't received your credentials.

Can I access EduVerse on mobile devices?

Yes! EduVerse is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Simply access it through your mobile browser.

How do I check my attendance?

After logging in, navigate to the "Attendance" section from the sidebar menu. You'll see your overall attendance percentage and subject-wise breakdown with detailed records.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive instructions to reset your password. Alternatively, contact your institution's IT support.

How do I pay my fees through EduVerse?

Go to the "Fee Management" section where you can view your fee structure, pending payments, and payment history. Click on "Pay Now" to proceed with online payment through our secure payment gateway.

Can parents track their child's progress?

Absolutely! Parents have access to their child's attendance records, academic performance, fee status, and can receive notifications about important updates and announcements.

Is my data secure on EduVerse?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. EduVerse uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal information. All data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.

Who do I contact for technical support?

For technical issues, contact your institution's IT help desk. You can also use the "Help" section in your dashboard to submit support tickets or access user guides.
